About The Game

Mafia Mystery is a real-time, multiplayer social deduction game inspired by "Wolfville" and "Mafia." Players are divided into two main factions—Mafia and Town—where deception, strategy, and social interaction drive the game.

Engage in intense discussions, form alliances, and use your detective skills to identify the villains or perfect your poker face to deceive the town.

Game Roles

Sheriff

Investigates one player each night to determine if they're suspicious.

Doctor

Protects one player from being eliminated each night.

Vigilante

Has the ability to eliminate one player during the game.

Godfather

Leads the Mafia and appears innocent to investigations.

Framer

Makes innocent players appear suspicious to investigations.

Consigliere

Can learn the exact role of one player each night.

Jester

Wins if they get voted out by the town.

Executioner

Wins if their target gets eliminated by the town.

Serial Killer

Works alone, can eliminate one player each night, and wins by being the last one standing.

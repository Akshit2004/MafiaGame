About The Game

Mafia Mystery is a real-time, multiplayer social deduction game inspired by "Wolfville" and "Mafia." Players are divided into two main factions—Mafia and Town—where deception, strategy, and social interaction drive the game.

Engage in intense discussions, form alliances, and use your detective skills to identify the villains or perfect your poker face to deceive the town.

Game Roles

Discover the intricate web of abilities, deception, and strategy in Mafia Mystery. Each role offers a unique gameplay experience with special powers that can change the course of the game.

Town

Work together to identify and eliminate the Mafia. Victory comes when all threats to the town are eliminated.

Investigative ⭐⭐

Sheriff

Investigates one player each night to determine if they're suspicious.

Night Ability: Select a player to learn if they are suspicious (Mafia, Serial Killer) or innocent.
Protective ⭐⭐⭐

Doctor

Protects one player from being eliminated each night.

Night Ability: Select a player to protect from a single attack that night.
Killing ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Vigilante

Has the ability to eliminate one player during the game.

Night Ability: Choose to shoot a player. Limited to 3 bullets.
Support ⭐⭐⭐

Mayor

Can reveal themselves to gain additional voting power.

Day Ability: Reveal role to gain 3x voting power for the rest of the game.
Investigative ⭐⭐⭐

Investigator

Gathers clues about a player's possible roles.

Night Ability: Investigate a player to get a list of possible roles they might be.
Support ⭐⭐⭐

Medium

Communicates with eliminated players at night.

Night Ability: Speak with dead players to gather information.
Investigative ⭐⭐⭐

Lookout

Watches who visits a player during the night.

Night Ability: See who visits your target during the night.

Mafia

Work together to eliminate the Town while maintaining your cover. You know your fellow Mafia members and can communicate secretly at night.

Leader ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Godfather

Leads the Mafia and appears innocent to investigations.

Night Ability: Order kills and appears innocent to Sheriff investigations.
Deception ⭐⭐⭐

Framer

Makes innocent players appear suspicious to investigations.

Night Ability: Frame a player to appear suspicious to Sheriff investigations.
Investigative ⭐⭐⭐

Consigliere

Can learn the exact role of one player each night.

Night Ability: Investigate one player to learn their exact role.
Killing ⭐⭐⭐

Mafioso

Carries out the Godfather's orders.

Night Ability: Kill targets as directed by the Godfather.
Disruptive ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Blackmailer

Silences a player for the next day.

Night Ability: Blackmail a player, preventing them from talking the next day.
Deception ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Janitor

Cleans up crime scenes to hide information.

Night Ability: Clean a dead body to hide the role from being revealed.

Neutral

Play for yourself with unique win conditions that aren't aligned with either Town or Mafia. Each neutral role follows their own path to victory.

Chaos ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Jester

Wins if they get voted out by the town.

Win Condition: Get yourself lynched by the town vote.
Manipulation ⭐⭐⭐

Executioner

Wins if their target gets eliminated by the town.

Win Condition: Get your target lynched by town vote.
Killing ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Serial Killer

Works alone, can eliminate one player each night.

Night Ability: Kill one player each night. Win by eliminating everyone.
Killing ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Arsonist

Douses players and ignites them all at once.

Night Ability: Douse players with gasoline or ignite all doused targets.
Control ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Witch

Controls other players' actions at night.

Night Ability: Force a player to use their ability on another player of your choice.
Survival ⭐⭐

Survivor

Just wants to survive until the end.

Night Ability: Use a bulletproof vest to protect yourself (limited uses).
